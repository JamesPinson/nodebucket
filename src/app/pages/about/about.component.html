Why Nodebucket Was Created!

We created Nodebucket to help those who always seem to be overwhelmed with task. Now you can organize your task and focus on getting things done. We designed an easy to use application for all users to keep track of all things going on in their lives. You can use Nodebucket for work, for school, or even household chores. The goal is to track your progress and see yourself reaching your goals. We hope this application brings value to your life just like it has ours. Thanks from the Nodebucket Team!
